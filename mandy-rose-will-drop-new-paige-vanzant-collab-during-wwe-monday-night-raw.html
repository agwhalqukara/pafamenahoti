Mandy Rose will drop new Paige VanZant collab during WWE Monday Night RAW

Publish date: 2024-07-11

Former WWE star Mandy Rose and former UFC star Paige VanZant have found success outside the realm of combat sports, amassing large followings on platforms like OnlyFans.

Recently, Rose took to Twitter to tease fans with an announcement prior to an upcoming WWE Monday Night RAW event.

She hinted at a collaboration with VanZant, which would be revealed on Monday, and fans quickly flooded the comments section with mixed reactions, well aware that this partnership will make waves on the internet.

The latest Mandy Rose and Paige VanZant collaboration

Rose has earned over a million dollars through explicit content, and VanZant has followed suit, quickly surpassing the WWE wrestler. However, no one could have anticipated that these two would join forces.

Over the weekend, Rose took to Twitter and shared a video featuring herself and VanZant in bikinis, announcing that the full content would be released on June 5. She urged fans not to miss it.

The post received various responses from fans. One believed that the release of the content during the airing of Monday Night RAW would cause many to switch their attention to Rose and VanZant.

"Mandy knows what she's doing, dropping it at the beginning of Monday Night Raw!!!" the fan wrote.

Another added: "That will blow the internet up..."

The video gained significant attention worldwide, with the former UFC fighter even sharing it on her Instagram profile and attracting a large audience.
