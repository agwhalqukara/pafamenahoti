Ram Kapoor is an Indian television and film actor who is widely known for his appearances in shows like Bade Achhe Lagte Hain, Kya Hua Tera Vaada, Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi, Kasamh Se, and many others. He is one of the most popular and highest-paid actors on Indian television. Having spent more than 2 decades in the television industry, Ram Kapoor is a household name and has won various awards for his performances.

    Ram Kapoor Facts

  • He and his wife Gautami met on the sets of Ghar Ek Mandir in 2002 in which Gautami played Ram’s sister-in-law.
  • His father passed away on April 12, 2021, after fighting a long battle with cancer.
  • Ram has been on television for more than 2 decades.
  • He made his debut with the show Nyaay in 1997.
  • He smoked more than 50 cigarettes a day but quit because of his daughter.
  • Ram Kapoor is the only actor on Indian television to do a kissing scene on screen.
  • His career changed completely after the hit show Bade Achhe Lagte Hain in 2011.
  • He is the only television actor to win the Indian Telly Awards consecutively 3 times in a row.
  • In July 2021, Ram Kapoor had purchased a sports car which was a blue Porsche 911 Carrera S worth INR 1.8 crore.
  • In November 2022, Ram Kapoor bought a Ferrari Portofino car worth INR 3.5 crore. It is a convertible grand tourer known for its motorsport legacy. At that time, he also owned a Harley Davidson bike.
