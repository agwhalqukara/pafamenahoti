Mandy Rose Husband: Who Is Tino Sabbatelli?

Publish date: 2024-09-10

Tino Sabbatelli is a former NXT superstar who fought under the WWE until his release in 2020.

Tony also played in the NFL from 2007 to 2011, with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers, Cleveland Browns, and Kansas City Chiefs.

He began dating Rose in 2018. The duo now run a fitness program and train people on physical wellness.

Who Is Mandy Rose?

Mandy Rose, whose real name is Amanda Rose Saccomanno, is a WWE wrestling champion, TV personality, and fitness competitor.

Born on July 18, 1990, in New York, Rose graduated from Iona College, where she studied speech pathology.

Rose began her fitness journey in 2013, at the World Bodybuilding Fitness & Fashion Boston Show.

She continued to take part in bodybuilding competitions, and won the World Beauty Fitness & Fashion Bikini Championship in 2014.

Rose later signed a deal with WWE, and in 2021, she Rose faced Raquel González and won her first career title – the NXT Women’s Champion.

The Toxic Attraction leader has gone on to participate in other championships such as WrestleMania and WWE Tough Enough.

Rose has also appeared in the reality television show Total Divas, and has appeared on the covers of many fitness magazines including Fitness Gurls, Fit & Firm, and FitFemme.
