Introduction :

Aly Michalka Stephen Ringer Relation :

Early Profession and Career :

  • Ringer began his film industry career in 2009 with the short film El Americano. The film was his first taste of cinematography, and he worked as a camera grip.
  • He worked on multiple films in 2009, including the shorts Page Four Hundred and Thirty-Nine and One Million Strong, both of which had him working as a gaffer.
  • He also became a full-fledged cinematographer, which is someone who leads the camera and electrical departments, on two films: a documentary short film called No Kill, and a short film called Little Richard.
  • Stephen ended the year with the short film On Your Pretty Dress, a drama and mystery film about a photographer who grows close to a client’s mother after the model disappears before a scheduled shoot.
  • He worked as both a camera operator and as the film’s cinematographer.

Work as a Cinematographer and Net Worth :

  • While Ringer continued to work as a gaffer and camera grip for many other films, he also began to spend more time as a cinematographer.
  • In 2011, he worked as the cinematographer for the short films The Argument, about a Frenchman in New Orleans who is trying to get past the language barrier to buy diapers for his son, and Snakes, about a father-son fishing trip in Idaho.
  • In 2012, he was the cinematographer for short films Two Summers Ago and The Return, and in 2014, he worked on a full-length film starring his now-wife Michalka called Sequoia. It remains one of his best works.
  • His last short film to date was The Films of Avi Krum.

Director and Writer :

  • As he began to work on more films, Ringer eventually became a director, which he did alongside his cinematography work.
  • He directed On Your Pretty Dress, Two Summers Ago, which he also wrote the screenplay for, and Leighton Meester: Heartstrings.
