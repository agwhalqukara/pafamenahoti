Is Kris Jenner Creating a Reality Dating Show For Khloe?

Publish date: 2024-07-13

In perhaps the oddest reality news TV since ‘I Dream of Nene’ on Bravo, there are rumors circulating that Kris Jenner is looking to create a new reality dating series for her freshly single daughter Khloe Kardashian. To now one’s surprise, HollywoodLife is claiming that the minute Khloe filed for divorce Kris’ wheels started spinning on how to capitalize on the situation.

Before you start penciling time into your schedule every Thursday night to watch the show we have to acknowledge that HollywoodLife is a webloid that has no problem publishing false stories. However, there are various sources that have said Kris believes that the best remedy for Khloe to get over her divorce would be a dating reality show. After all these years we finally find out what the cure for heart break is thanks to Kris Jenner.

Right now, we have no reason to believe this is going to happen, but it’s Kris Jenner.  Of all the Kardashian sisters, Khloe doesn’t strike us as one who would be up for something like this. We will keep you posted if anything materializes.
