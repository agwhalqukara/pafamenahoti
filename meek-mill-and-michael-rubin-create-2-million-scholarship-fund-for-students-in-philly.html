Meek Mill And Michael Rubin Create $2 Million Scholarship Fund For Students In Philly

Publish date: 2024-09-30

It’s been just revealed that Meek Meek and Michael Rubin are giving back to the community ahead of the holidays. Check out the latest reports coming from The Shade Room. 

TSR began by mentioning the fact that the school systems have been some of the most neglected parts of our society.

More than that, it’s pretty obvious the fact that this terrible pandemic has definitely added some pressure for districts to provide resources for students.

Meek Mill is working hard to change all this, and this time, he had help from Michael Rubin, another Philly native.

TSR cites info from TMZ and notes that ‘Meek and 76ers partner #MichaelRubin have created their own scholarship fund to help underprivileged youth in Philadelphia! The fund is expected to help nearly 1,000 students in the Philly school system and is expected to be distributed immediately.’

‘A source close to the situation tells TMZ that the money will allow students from poverty-stricken homes to attend private schools throughout the city,’ TSR made sure to note as well.
