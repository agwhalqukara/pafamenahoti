Publish date: 2024-07-27

Uebert Angel is a British-Zimbabwean charismatic preacher man and founder of Spirit Embassy in the United Kingdom. He was born in an African country called Zimbabwe on the 4th of March 1972.

Uebert Angel, formerly known as Hubert Mudzanire, is the Presidential Envoy and Ambassador  At Large for the people of Zimbabwe to Europe and the Americas. He is very popular in all parts of the world due to the fact that he has established branches in some countries. Spirit Embassy church has a branch in Germany, South Africa, Zambia, Namibia, and his native land Zimbabwe.
